Key Concepts

Training Data

The labeled examples (mineral samples) used to teach the AI model. More diverse data leads to better learning.

Features

The measurable characteristics of each sample (color, density, etc.) that the model uses to learn patterns.

Labels

The correct answers (mineral types) for each sample that help the model learn what to look for.

Space Fact

How the AI Learns

The AI starts by making random guesses (initial decision boundary). Through training, it:

  1. Makes predictions on training data
  2. Calculates how wrong it was (loss)
  3. Adjusts its parameters to reduce mistakes
  4. Repeats until it performs well

Gradient Descent

The optimization algorithm that helps the AI adjust its parameters, like a spaceship navigating to the lowest point in a crater.
